Research Interests

My core interest lies in the broad fields of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Deep Learning for a variety of applications. I'm passionate about is using AI/NLP/DL methods to solve real-world problems, by creating systems for AI-assisted Software Engineering and healthcare.

As humans we have the ability to learn new concepts, ideas, and skills quickly and effectively. We use our learnings from one task to learn decisions for others. My long term goal is to build AI systems that can do the same. Some interesting research domains that help us do this revolve around learning to learn - Meta Learning.

Some other related domains and concepts that I like to think about and interest me are - Bayesian Inference, Multi-task learning, Probabilistic view of meta-learning, Machine Learning Theory and Computational Neuro-Science

If you'd like to collaborate on research, chat with me about AI, or get free advice then drop me an email.
