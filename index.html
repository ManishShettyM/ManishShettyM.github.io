Research Interests

Software Engineering | Machine Learning | Systems (ML4Sys & Sys4ML)

Currently, my research focusses on combining neural and traditional techniques to build novel systems that aid software engineering processes.

> From a systems/SE perspective, I'm fascinated by how data-driven programs are changing how we develop software and its effects - Empirical SE, ML4SE, Program Synthesis, MLOps.

> From a machine learning perspective, I'm excited about techniques that allow processing weak, dynamic, and reusable stimuli - Multi-task Learning, Meta Learning, Weak/Distant Supervision.
