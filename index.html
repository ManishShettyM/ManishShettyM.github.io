Research Interests

Software Engineering (SE) | Machine Learning (ML) | Systems (Sys)

Currently, my research focusses on combining statistical, neural, and traditional techniques to build novel systems that improve/aid software engineering processes.

From a SE/Sys perspective, I'm fascinated by how distributed data-driven programs are changing how we develop, maintain, debug, and evolve software systems - Empirical SE, ML+SE, ML+Sys, Program Analysis & Synthesis.

From an ML perspective, I'm excited about techniques that allow processing weak, dynamic, and reusable stimuli - Meta/Multi-task Learning, Weak/Programmatic Supervision

If you'd like to collaborate or just chat with me about about research, feel free to drop me an email.
