Research Interests

My core interest lies broadly in the intersections of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Information Extraction. In particular, I am interested in the extraction and representation of knowledge for automated reasoning and inference. I'm currently working on structured information and knowledge extraction for AI-assisted Software Engineering @ Microsoft.

As humans we have the ability to learn new concepts, ideas, and skills quickly and effectively. We use knowledge learnt from one task to learn decisions for others. We also effectively process noisy and complex real-world stimuli. A long term goal of my research is to build AI systems that can do the same. Some interesting research domains, that revolve around this, that I'd like to explore are:

  • Multi-task Learning - Exploiting shared representation across related tasks. [e.g. SoftNER]
  • Meta Learning - Learning to Learn
  • Neuro-Science ðŸ”„AI - Modelling how the brain solves challenges ↔ Better AI algorithms.
  • Robust & Interpretable ML - Modelling for low resource/ long tailed data

If you'd like to collaborate, chat with me about AI, or just know about research in general, feel free to drop me an email.
