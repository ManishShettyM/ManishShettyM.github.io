I am a PhD student in the Programming Systems group at UC Berkeley advised by Koushik Sen. I broadly research techniques and design systems to quickly analyze and synthesize programs.

My research is in the intersection of programming systems (PL & SE) and data systems. I'm specifically studying the connections between programs and graphs to develop efficient and intuitive approaches to program analysis, synthesis, and optimization. By leveraging the graph structure of programs and the mathematical properties of graphs and their algorithms, I aim to get deeper insights into the programs and their behavior.

Previously, I worked at Microsoft Research (MSR) with Chetan Bansal, Suman Nath, and Tom Zimmermann on developer and debugging tools for cloud reliability.
