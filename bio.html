I am a PhD student in the Programming Systems group at UC Berkeley. I'm broadly interested in designing systems, powered by predictive intelligence, to improve the productivity of programmers and non-programmers interacting with data.

My research is in the intersection of Programming Systems (PL & SE) and Data Systems. I'm exploring predictive tools that can simplify how we—both programmers and non-programmers—develop, interact, and make decisions with data at scale using program analysis, synthesis, and learning. Towards this, my goal involves better combining empirical, neural, and symbolic methods to build intelligent systems.

Previously, I worked at Microsoft Research (MSR) with Chetan Bansal, Suman Nath, and Tom Zimmermann on developer and debugging tools for cloud reliability.
